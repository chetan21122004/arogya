About Arogya Yatri

Your Trusted Partner in Global Medical Care

Arogya Yatri is built to simplify international medical travel by combining ethical guidance, world-class hospitals, and complete patient support — so you can focus on healing with confidence.

Multilingual Support

Powered by EWAN Business Solutions, we enable smooth communication in 120+ languages — ensuring you feel understood, supported, and cared for throughout your journey.

Save Up to 80% on Treatment

Access world-class treatment in India at a fraction of Western and Gulf costs, without compromising on quality, safety, or hospital accreditation.

Post-Treatment Follow-up & Medicine Delivery

Even after you return home, we stay connected with your doctors and ensure prescribed medicines are delivered safely to your doorstep.

Companion Travel Assistance*

Bring a loved one along. We assist with companion travel arrangements including visa and documentation, making your journey more comfortable.

Complete Start-to-End Assistance

From medical care and language support to travel, stay, and tourism — Arogya Yatri is your single point of contact throughout the journey.

Free Report Analysis & Guidance

Share your medical reports for a free, no-obligation review by our expert panel and receive transparent treatment recommendations.

Diet-Focused Care & Nutrition

Personalized diet and nutrition plans aligned with your treatment, cultural preferences, and recovery goals — supporting long-term health.
